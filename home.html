05   IndustriesWhere planning gets hard

Different industries.
The same need for clarity.

Demand planning is difficult in different ways depending on the environment. Haidi is built to handle the complexity behind each.

Pharma

Pharma

Tight regulation, expiry and shelf-life pressure, and long lead times make demand swings expensive. Forecasts have to be defensible, not just close.

Manufacturing

Manufacturing

Capacity limits, component dependencies, and supply constraints mean a forecast miss ripples straight into production and inventory.

FMCG
